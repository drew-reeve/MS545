General Assignment Outline and Guidelines

Published on July 7, 2025 • By Andrew Reeve

Each Post will address the following areas and questions for the week (per assignment requirements):

    Journal:

  • Make a detailed list of the tasks you performed this week for this class.
  • What work did you perform and what did you learn from doing those tasks?

    • Teachback:

  • What did you learn this week in class?
  • How does this information relate to things you have studied in other classes or earlier in this course?
  • What material stood out to you?
  • How did your view of the industry / job of producer change based on what you learned?

    • Research and Analysis

  • Why is the information we covered in class important?
  • Why did we spend our valuable time learning it?
  • How do you plan to use it in the future?
  • What information would you add to this lesson to gain additional insight on the topic?

    • Guidelines and Expectations

    The Journal will take the form of an essay/blog on my experience from a Leadership, Strategy, and/or Production perspective.
